Welcome to the **Community Service Scheduler** information page.

## About This Site

This site serves to organize and inform about community service schedules. It is managed collaboratively by all participants.

### Service Schedules

The service schedules are managed by the respective weekday groups themselves. **Each weekday group is self-organizing!**

:::note[Note]
This is a template/demo project. Customize the content to fit your organization's needs.
:::

### Contact Persons

The [contact persons list](/docs/ansprechpartnerinnen) contains contact information for coordinators and volunteers.

## Service Times

Service takes place **Monday through Friday** from **1:00 PM to 2:00 PM**.

## Coordination

The coordination is handled by the designated program coordinator.

For technical questions about this website, contact your technical administrator.
